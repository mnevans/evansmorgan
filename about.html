Hello, I'm Morgan.

I'm a Denver-based UX designer with additional background in front-end development and product management. I graduated from the University of Washington with a B.S. in Informatics and a focus in Human Computer Interaction (and a minor in French).

Currently I'm a Sr. Experience Designer at Adobe where I've spent the past several years working across all platforms of Adobe's premier digital imaging products. As of recent I'm helping a small but mighty team of designers expand Photoshop to the web.

I've worked across all surfaces of creative imaging products as both a designer and engineer. Along the way I earned a Certificate in Product Management from the Haas School of Business, and consider myself involved, educated, and passionate about all parts of a product team. I love bringing ideas to life via prototyping and work quickly to get my team's work in front of customers.

When I'm not working I enjoy taking advantage of the vast trails that the Rocky Mountains offer, skiing, traveling, practicing yoga, and playing with my cat Fiona.
