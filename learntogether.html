LEARN TOGETHER MOBILE APPLICATION

Team Size: 3 | Role: UX Designer | Software Used: Sketch, Photoshop, InVision | Date: January 2016

background

Learn Together is a startup focused on the social learning of soft skills (e.g. customer service, leadership, collaboration). It is partnered with customer service-centered companies to help teach their employees how to improve upon their soft skills via direct mentorship with other employees and Learn Together users.

objective


The objective of this project was to create a high fidelity prototype for the Learn Together Version 2 mobile application. The goal was to make it simple, intuitive and focused on helping companies improve their customer service with a social networking component.

stakeholders


The primary stakeholder for this service is Starbucks to train their baristas in customer service. Others are Alaska Air to aid their airplane stewards, Costco and REI.

research + use cases


Refine the findings and conceptual models to establish the key functionalities and look and feel, then transform it all into a hi fi prototype.

first iteration


Bring the designs to life and give them working interactions and features, while focusing on providing the same aesthetics and user experience.

second iteration


Present the products to the stakeholders, project team, etc., and allow them to critique the work and eventually move it to testing (usability studies, A/B testing, etc.)

final iteration


Once user feedback has been received and analyzed, go back to whichever step is necessary and do it all again! Iterate. Iterate. Iterate.
