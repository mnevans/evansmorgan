Bonjour, I'm Morgan.

I'm a Denver-based UX designer, with additional background in front-end development and product management. I graduated from the University of Washington in 2016 with a B.S. in Informatics and a focus in Human Computer Interaction (and a minor in French), and am currently working as a Sr. Experience Designer at Adobe.

My passion is collaborating with and serving as the primary point of communcation between product managers, designers and engineers to bring an idea and vision to life. Having experience in all of these fields, I pride myself in my understanding and ability to empathize with the work at hand to produce innovative, intuitive and beautiful products.

When I'm not working, I enjoy taking advantage of the vast hiking trails that the Rocky Mountains offer, skiing, traveling, practicing yoga, and playing with my cat Fiona.
